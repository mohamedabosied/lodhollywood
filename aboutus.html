Our Story

Our story began when we discovered the need for debt follow-up services and financial problems for international companies that export to global financial services - and the field of managing international debt portfolios (Egypt), and then we established an Egyptian company specialized in collecting debts on an international scale. At that time, banks and companies in Egypt preferred to rely on their employees to manage their proceeds and accounts and did not want to deal with foreign companies, which changed after a few years and the Egyptian management methods changed. The first goal was to solve the financial problems facing foreign exporting companies with Egyptian importers, then expanded by managing international debt portfolios in the field of credit risk and adding strategic relationships with international groups.
